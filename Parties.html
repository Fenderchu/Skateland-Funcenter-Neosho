Public Parties

(Requires a down payment of $20 at the time of booking or you can pay in full)

Party Room an additional $10 plus tax
Additional skaters $5.00 plus tax each
Saturday night party available from 7p.m-9p.m.
Party room already included for $100 plus tax
Additional skaters $6.00 plus tax
Receives one pitcher of soda for party guests

☑ May bring in cake, cupcakes, ice cream
