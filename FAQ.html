FAQ

Frequently Asked Questtions


Question 1:
How much are inlines and speed skates?

Answer 1:
$3.00


Question 2:
How much are skatemates?

Answer 2:
$4.00


Question 3:
What forms of payment do you take?

Answer 3:
We take cash, checks, and card or apple pay. Using a card or apple pay does have a small service charge.


Question 4:
How far in advance do I need to book a party?

Answer 4:
Most public parties may be booked within 2 weeks of the party date. Private parties need to be booked at least 2 months in advance. This allows you to choose the date you were wanting. Private parties are much more limited and book up much quicker.


Question 5:
May I walk on the floor with my child?

Answer 5:
Yes, but stay by the wall at all times.


Question 6:
May I request a song?

Answer 6:
Yes, only if it is appropriate.


Question 7:
May I bring in my own skates?

Answer 7:
Yes. We must check them to make sure they are safe for our skate floor.


Question 8:
May I come in without paying?

Answer 8:
If you are younger than 18 without a guardian staying, then you must pay. If you are 18 or older or have a guardian staying, then you do not have to pay.


Question 9:
May I step outside?

Answer 9:
We do not allow anyone under the age of 18 outside our doors unless they drove here and are leaving. If you a smoke break, you must be at least the legal age of 21. No skates may be worn outside even if they belong to you.


Question 10:
May I use this free or BOGO skate pass?

Answer 10:
On our normal operating hours you may. If we have a special session, we do not accept any passes.


Question 11:
Do you do school, home school, church skates?

Answer 11:
We can, those would have to be booked with Cathy by whoever oversees the event. These are different costs depending on what is or is not included.


Question 12:
How long have you been in business?

Answer 12:
The building was built in 1988 and was open on Friday, January 13th 1989.
